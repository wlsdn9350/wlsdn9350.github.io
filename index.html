Projects
SNUVI: Autonomous Vehicle for Urban Driving
Role: Team leader, local path planning

SNUVI is an autonomous vehicle developed for urban driving. It integrates precise perception, localization, and decision-making systems to handle pedestrians, traffic, and road rules, and was evaluated in diverse urban scenarios at K-City in South Korea.

VISKY: Mobile Robot for Autonomous Exploration in Unstructured Environments
Role: Project Manager, global path planning

VISKY is a mobile robot built for autonomous exploration in unknown, unstructured, and hazardous environments, relying on robust perception, accurate SLAM, and real-time path replanning.
